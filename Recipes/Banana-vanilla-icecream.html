Banana-vanilla ice cream

Nutritional benefits of the Banana-vanilla ice cream

If you are looking for a great way to introducing fruits to your kids, or trying to break their resistence to fuits, the recipe is a great option. Ice cream is known for being one of kids favorite foods, but is also a bomb of sugar. In this case we have a fruit based and low sugar version, that can satisfy your kid's desire to sweets and also help them eat more fruits

A delicious and low sugar desert for children!

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 2 table spoons of powder milk
  • 5 drops of vanilla essence
  • sweetener of your preference

Steps

  1. With a mixer or blender, mix the banana, the powder milk and the vanilla essence until it becomes an ice cream like mix
