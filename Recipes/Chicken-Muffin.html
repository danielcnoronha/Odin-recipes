Chicken Muffin

Nutritional benefits of the chicken muffin

By Combining chicken and eggs, the chickken muffin is an excellent choice to start introducing high protein meals to your child's diet. The appearance of a muffin is a lot more attractive to children then you regular chicken breast and eggs making protein introduction easier!

A delicious and healthy snack for children!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 3 spoons of tapioca
  • Combining chicken and eggs
  • 1 thin slice of cheese
  • Salt as needed

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add all the ingredients and mix them well
  2. Put the mix in silicone or glass molds
  3. Put them in the airfryer in 180 degrees celsius for 20 minutes or until cooked
  4. It can also be cooked in the oven, but the cooking time changes
